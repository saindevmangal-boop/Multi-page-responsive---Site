About Maincraft

www.maincrafts.com — hr@maincrafts.com

Maincraft is a demo site used in the Full Stack Web Development Internship tasks. It demonstrates client-side interactivity, LocalStorage usage, and accessible form handling.

Our Mission

Privacy-first

Local-only storage keeps submissions on the user's device unless they choose to share.

Accessible

Semantic markup, focus states, and clear error messages improve usability for everyone.

Lightweight

No backend required for demo flows; everything runs in plain HTML, CSS and JS.
